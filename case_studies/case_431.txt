Counselor: Your feelings of sadness and loss are entirely normal, given the circumstances. It's important to acknowledge these emotions and allow yourself to feel them fully. This is where mindfulness and acceptance come into play. By practicing mindfulness, you can stay present with your emotions, allowing them to ebb and flow without judgment. Acceptance, on the other hand, is about acknowledging the reality of the situation and finding peace with it.
