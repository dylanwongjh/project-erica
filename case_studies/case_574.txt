=== CASE STUDY PROFILE ===
Topic: Anticipatory grief and pandemic-related bereavement

Caregiver Profile: Female, likely in her 50s or older, married, experiencing loss of a spouse due to COVID-19 restrictions.
Clinical Goal: To help the client process complex emotions related to sudden loss, find ways to cope with grief during the pandemic, and explore long-term support options.

Underlying Need: Emotional safety and control

=== COMMUNICATION TECHNIQUES USED ===
Active listening, Validation, Reframing (acknowledging the client's feelings and experiences while offering alternative perspectives), Empathy, Problem-focused coping (encouraging self-care practices).

User: I've been feeling quite depressed lately... My husband passed away suddenly last month. The grief I feel is immense, and I've been struggling to find ways to cope.
