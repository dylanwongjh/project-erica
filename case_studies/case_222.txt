Topic: Managing Caregiver Burden in End-of-Life Care

Clinical Goal: To explore alternatives to medicating the wife and to manage caregiver burden while maintaining her dignity and comfort.

Underlying Need: Emotional safety and control in caregiving situation

=== COMMUNICATION TECHNIQUES USED ===
