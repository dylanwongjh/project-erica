Clinical Goal: To help the patient manage the emotional challenge of downsizing and moving, while acknowledging and processing grief related to her mother's passing.
Summary: A female caregiver is struggling to cope with the emotional aftermath of caring for her mother with Alzheimer's, feeling overwhelmed by the process of sorting through belongings and preparing to move to a new home.

Primary Emotions: Grief, Overwhelm, Sadness

=== COMMUNICATION TECHNIQUES USED ===

- Validation (recognizing the patient's feelings and experiences)
- Reframing negative thoughts (helping the patient reframe the process as an opportunity for growth and healing)
- Visualization (encouraging the patient to focus on the end goal of a clutter-free new home)
- Breaking down tasks into manageable steps (to reduce feelings of overwhelm)
